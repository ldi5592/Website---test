Wheather its species, people or places, WWF is working hard to support and provide for things that need it. tigers, lions and hippos are just some examples of species that WWF is working to protect. Forest, fresh water and oceans come under places that we are trying to protect. We chose which locations to help based on the wealth and variety of life they support, the destructive challenges they face, and our ability to positively impact them.
